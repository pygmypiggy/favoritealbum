Amnesiac

By:

Radiohead

About:

The fifth studio album released by Radiohead
just one year after their critically acclaimed album "Kid A".
Amnesiac is an alternative album like Kid A,
but with a darker and jazzier sound.

Band Members:

  • Thom Yorke - Lead vocals, Piano

  • Jonny Greenwood - Multiple instruments

  • Ed O'Brien - Background vocals, Guitar

  • Colin Greenwood - Bass guitar

  • Philip Selway - Drums
